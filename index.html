3 REASONS WHY YOU SHOULD TRY SCUBA DIVING

1. YOU HAVE THE CHANCE TO DIVE WITH MARINE CREATURES!

There will be encounters with sea creatures that you have never seen before, and you can see them at a very close distance.

2. TO BECOME AN UNDERWATER EXPLORER

70% of the earth surface is covered by water. You will discover the new face of this planet.

3. TO FEEL THE WEIGHTLESSNESS AND NO DISTRACTION

I personally believe there is no better form of escapism than diving. While diving, all you focus is your breathing (meditation) and monitor your gauges as well as your buddy (being in the present).
